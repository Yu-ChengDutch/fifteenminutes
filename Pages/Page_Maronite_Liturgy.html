Credo

Nuʾminu bi-ʾilāh wāḥid, Ābin ḍābiṭi-l-kulli, khāliqi s-samā’i wal-arḍi, kulli-mā yurā wa-mā lā yurā.
Wa-bi-Rabbin wāḥidin Yasūʿa l-Masīḥa, ibn-Allāhi l-wāḥidi, al-mawlūdi min-l-Ābi qabl kulli-d-duhūr, nūrin min-nūr, ʾilāhan ḥaqqan min-ʾilāhin ḥaqq, mawlūdan ghayra mukhlūqin, musāwin lil-Ābi fi-l-jawhar, ḏālika bi-hi kāna kulli shayin.
Alladhī min ʾajlinā naḥnu l-bashar wa-min ʾajli khalaṣinā nazala mimma s-samā’i, wa-tajassada min-r-Rūḥi l-Qudusi wa-Maryami l-ʿAdhrā’i, wa-tannaʾasa.
Wa-ṣuliba ʿannā ʿalā ʿahdi Bīlāṭusi l-Buntaṭī, wa-taʾallama wa-qūbira, wa-qāma fī l-yawm l-thālithi ʿalā mā fī-l-kutubi.
Wa-ṣaʿida ilā s-samāwāti, wa-jalasa ʿan ymīni-l-Ābi, wa-ayḍan yāti bi-majdin li-yadīna l-aḥyā’a wal-amwāta. Alladhī lā fanyana li-malakihi.
Wa-bi-r-Rūḥi l-Qudusi, l-Rabbi l-muḥyi, l-munbaṯiqi min-l-Ābi, alladhī huwa maʿa-l-Ābi wa-l-Ibn, musjaduⁿ lahu wa-mumajjadu, l-nāṭiqi bi-l-anbiyā’i.
Wa-bi-kanisatin wāḥidatin, ġāmʿatin, muqaddaśatin, rusūliyatin.
Wa-aʿtarifu bi-maʿmūḏiyyatin wāḥidatin li-maghfirati l-khaṭāyā, wa-antarja qiyāmata l-mawtā wa-l-ḥayāta fī d-dahr l-āti. Āmīn.

Mhymanīn ḥnan b-ḥad Alāhā ʾĀbā ḥayelṯānā, brīyā d-šmayyā w-arʿā w-kol ḥezayyā w-lā ḥezayyā.
W-b-ḥad Moran Yeshūʿ Mshiḥā, brēh yaḥidayā d-Alāhā, dīleḏ men ʾĀbā qadm kolhon ʿalmīn; nuhrā men nuhrā, Alāhā shrīrā men Alāhā shrīrā, yelīd wlō ʿbīd, ḥad b-kyānā ʿam ʾĀbā; w-bīdeh hwā kol madēm.
D-metolten bnaynoshā w-metol purqānān neḥaṯ men šmayyā, w-etgasham men Rūḥā d-Qūdshā w-men Maryam btultā w-hwā barnāš.
W-etzaḏaq ḥlapān b-yomnā d-Ponṭyos Pilāṭos, w-eshtaʿel w-etqbar, w-qām b-yomā d-tlātā ayk d-kṯābē.
W-slaq l-šmayyā, w-yateb men ymīnā d-ʾĀbā, w-tūb ʾātē b-šūbḥā d-nadūn ḥayyē w-mītē, d-malkūteh lēt leh ḥrātā.
W-b-Rūḥā d-Qūdshā, Moryo mḥayyō, d-nafq men ʾĀbā, d-ʿam ʾĀbā w-Brā mishtaḥḥīn leh w-mšabbaḥīn leh, d-mallel b-yad nḇyē.
W-b-ḥad ʿiḏtā qadīštā katholīqī w-šliḥāytā.
W-modīn ḥnan b-maʿmūdītā ḥdā l-šūbqan ḥaṭāhē.
W-msabbryn ḥnan qyāmṯā d-mītē, w-ḥayyē d-ʿalmā d-ātē. Amīn.

We believe in one God, the Father almighty, maker of heaven and earth, of all things visible and invisible.
And in one Lord Jesus Christ, the only-begotten Son of God, born of the Father before all ages, Light from Light, true God from true God, begotten not made, consubstantial with the Father; through Him all things were made.
For us men and for our salvation He came down from heaven, and by the Holy Spirit was incarnate of the Virgin Mary, and became man.
For our sake He was crucified under Pontius Pilate, He suffered death and was buried, and rose again on the third day in accordance with the Scriptures.
He ascended into heaven and is seated at the right hand of the Father. He will come again in glory to judge the living and the dead, and His kingdom will have no end.
And in the Holy Spirit, the Lord, the giver of life, who proceeds from the Father, who with the Father and the Son is adored and glorified, who has spoken through the prophets.
We believe in one, holy, catholic, and apostolic Church.
We confess one baptism for the forgiveness of sins, and we look forward to the resurrection of the dead and the life of the world to come. Amen.
